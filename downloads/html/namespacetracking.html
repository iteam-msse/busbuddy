Detailed Description

Tracking Module manages GPS vehicle tracking and alerting subscribed users based on vehicle location.

The Tracking Module handles the tracking GPS data from the transit vehicles and initiating alerts to users subscribed to route based messages and triggers transit vehicle delay alerts. The transit vehicle location data is also available to the Maps Module to place the bus icon at the correct position on maps.
